Plungers & Launch Devices
=========================

+------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Related Config File Sections                                                 |
+==============================================================================+

A Plunger is a type of ball device. MPF supports mechanical (traditional
"spring" plungers), coil-fired plungers, and combo auto/manual plungers.
